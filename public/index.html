Hedera Town Hall API

Description

    Features Include:
  • Retrieve information on all videos in the Town Hall playlist /api/videos


  • Retrieve all chapters from all available videos /api/videos/chapters

  • Retrieve all subtitles from all available videos /api/videos/subtitles

  • Retrieve all chapters and subtitles from all available videos /api/videos/all

  • Retrieve general information on a specific video in the Town Hall playlist*

  • Retrieve chapters of a specific video in the Town Hall playlist*

  • Retrieve subtitles of a specific video in the Town Hall playlist*

  • Provide a query to narrow down to a specific set of words or phrases



    • *note: There wasn't a town hall in December 2020
